Card War Game!


Track of Cards

Player1 Player2

Instruction

Code the card game, War. You may have played it before, but for the sake of this exercise, it looks like this. One deck of 52 cards is shuffled and dealt evenly between two players. During each play, each player lays down one card face up. The player with the higher ranking card (aces are high, suits (clubs, diamonds, spades, hearts) are ignored) takes both cards and add them to the bottom of his or her deck. If the cards are equal, a "war" ensues. The current cards are left in play. Each player plays another card face down, then another card face up. The player with the higher face up card takes all the cards that are in play and adds them to bottom of his or her deck. If the face up cards are still equal, the war continues until one player plays a higher card.
